Brioche

Recipe

  • 2 1/2 cups (300g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup (50g) sugar
  • 1/2 tsp (2.5g) salt
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup (120g) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) milk
  • 1 tbsp (10g) active dry yeast

Instructions

1. In a bowl, warm the milk and dissolve the yeast in it. Let sit for 5 minutes.
2. In a separate bowl, combine flour, sugar, and salt.
3. Add the yeast mixture and eggs to the flour mixture. Mix until a dough forms.
4. Add the softened butter a little at a time, mixing until fully incorporated.
5. Knead the dough until smooth and elastic, about 10 minutes.
6. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover, and let rise for 1-2 hours until doubled.
7. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
8. Shape the dough into a loaf or rolls and place in a greased pan.
9. Let the dough rise for another 30-45 minutes.
10. Bake for 25-30 minutes until golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
11. Cool completely before serving.
