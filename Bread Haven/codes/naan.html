Naan

Recipe

  • 2 cups (250g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp (5g) salt
  • 1 tsp (5g) sugar
  • 1 tsp (5g) baking powder
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) warm water
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) yogurt
  • 2 tbsp (30ml) melted butter or ghee

Instructions

1. In a bowl, combine the flour, salt, sugar, and baking powder.
2. Add the warm water, yogurt, and melted butter or ghee. Mix until a soft dough forms.
3. Knead the dough for 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
4. Cover and let rest for 30 minutes.
5. Preheat a skillet or griddle over high heat.
6. Divide the dough into equal portions and roll them into oval or teardrop shapes.
7. Cook the naan on the hot skillet for 1-2 minutes per side until lightly charred.
8. Brush with melted butter or ghee and serve hot.
