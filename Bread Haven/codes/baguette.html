Baguette

Recipe

  • 3 1/4 cups (400g) bread flour
  • 1 1/2 cups (350g) water
  • 1 tsp (5g) salt
  • 1/4 tsp (1g) instant yeast

Instructions

1. Combine flour, water, salt, and yeast in a bowl. Mix until a shaggy dough forms.
2. Cover and let rest for 30 minutes.
3. Knead the dough until smooth, about 10 minutes.
4. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover, and let rise for 1-2 hours until doubled.
5. Preheat oven to 475° F (245°C).
6. Shape the dough into baguettes and place on a floured cloth or parchment paper.
7. Let the baguettes rise for another 45 minutes.
8. Score the tops and bake for 20-25 minutes until golden brown.
9. Cool completely before slicing.
