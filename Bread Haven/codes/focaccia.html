Focaccia

Recipe

  • 3 cups (375g) bread flour
  • 1 1/4 cups (300ml) warm water
  • 1 tsp (5g) salt
  • 1 tsp (5g) instant yeast
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) olive oil
  • Toppings (e.g., rosemary, olives, tomatoes)

Instructions

1. In a bowl, combine the flour, water, salt, and yeast. Mix until a shaggy dough forms.
2. Cover and let rest for 1 hour.
3. Knead the dough for a few minutes, then place it in a lightly oiled bowl.
4. Cover and let rise for 1-2 hours until doubled in size.
5. Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C).
6. Stretch the dough into a greased baking pan and dimple the surface with your fingertips.
7. Drizzle with olive oil and add desired toppings.
8. Bake for 20-25 minutes until golden brown.
9. Cool slightly before slicing and serving.
