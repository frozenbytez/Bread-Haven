Ciabatta

Recipe

  • 3 cups (375g) bread flour
  • 1 1/4 cups (300ml) warm water
  • 1 tsp (5g) salt
  • 1/4 tsp (1g) instant yeast

Instructions

1. In a bowl, combine the flour, water, salt, and yeast. Mix until a shaggy dough forms.
2. Cover and let rest for 12-18 hours at room temperature.
3. Gently fold the dough a few times and let it rest for another 30 minutes.
4. Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C).
5. Shape the dough into oblong loaves and place on a floured surface.
6. Let the loaves rise for another 30-45 minutes.
7. Bake for 20-25 minutes until golden brown.
8. Cool completely before slicing.
