Whole Wheat

Recipe

  • 3 cups (360g) whole wheat flour
  • 1 1/2 cups (360g) water
  • 2 tbsp (30ml) honey
  • 2 tsp (10g) salt
  • 1 tbsp (10g) active dry yeast

Instructions

1. In a bowl, dissolve yeast in warm water with honey. Let it sit for 5 minutes.
2. Add flour and salt to the yeast mixture. Mix until combined.
3. Knead the dough for 10 minutes until smooth.
4. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover, and let rise for 1 hour until doubled.
5. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
6. Shape the dough into a loaf and place in a greased loaf pan.
7. Let the dough rise for another 30 minutes.
8. Bake for 30-35 minutes until golden brown and sounds hollow when tapped.
9. Cool completely before slicing.
