Rye Bread

Recipe

  • 2 cups (250g) rye flour
  • 1 1/2 cups (180g) bread flour
  • 1 1/4 cups (300ml) warm water
  • 2 tbsp (30ml) molasses
  • 1 tbsp (15g) caraway seeds
  • 2 tsp (10g) salt
  • 1 tsp (5g) active dry yeast

Instructions

1. In a bowl, dissolve yeast in warm water with molasses. Let it sit for 5 minutes.
2. In a separate bowl, combine rye flour, bread flour, caraway seeds, and salt.
3. Add the yeast mixture to the flour mixture. Mix until a dough forms.
4. Knead the dough for 10 minutes until smooth.
5. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover, and let rise for 1 hour until doubled.
6. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
7. Shape the dough into a loaf and place in a greased loaf pan.
8. Let the dough rise for another 30 minutes.
9. Bake for 30-35 minutes until golden brown and sounds hollow when tapped.
10. Cool completely before slicing.
