Sourdough

Recipe

  • 500g bread flour
  • 350ml water
  • 100g sourdough starter
  • 10g salt

Instructions

1. Mix the flour and water in a bowl. Let it rest for 30 minutes (autolyse).
2. Add the sourdough starter and salt. Mix until combined.
3. Knead the dough until smooth and elastic.
4. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover, and let rise for 3-4 hours, folding every 30 minutes.
5. Shape the dough and place in a floured banneton. Let it proof for 2-3 hours.
6. Preheat your oven to 250°C (482°F) with a Dutch oven inside.
7. Transfer the dough to the Dutch oven, score the top, and bake covered for 20 minutes.
8. Remove the lid and bake for another 20-25 minutes until golden brown.
9. Cool completely before slicing.
